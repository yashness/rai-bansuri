Our Story

Crafted with Passion, Rooted in Tradition

Rai Bansuri is born from a deep reverence for the Indian bamboo flute — the Bansuri. Each instrument is carefully handcrafted by Gaurab, who brings together generations of knowledge with a meticulous ear for tonal perfection.

From selecting the finest Assam bamboo to the final tuning of each note, every step is guided by an unwavering commitment to quality. Our flutes are not just instruments — they are vessels of Indian musical heritage.

The Craft

From Bamboo Grove to Musical Soul

01

Selection

Hand-picking mature Assam bamboo with the ideal density, grain, and resonance for superior tonal quality.

02

Shaping

Carefully boring, shaping, and smoothing the bamboo shaft to achieve the perfect internal diameter and airflow.

03

Tuning

Precision hole placement and fine-tuning each note to concert pitch (A=440Hz) with chromatic accuracy.

04

Finishing

Thread binding, natural oil treatment, and engraving the Rai Bansuri mark — ready to sing.
